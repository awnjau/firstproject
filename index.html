Books I've Read in 2021

On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous by Ocean Vuong 4.5/5 stars A beautifully written memoir detailing the trials and tribulations of being a gay Vietnamese man and son. You can easily tell he's a poet.His complicated relationship with his mother and his first love is raw and emotional. With outstanding prose and many quote-worthy moments, this book will leave you questioning your own upbringing.

A Court of Thorns and Roses(The First Three Books)by Sarah J Mass 2/5 stars for the whole series The first book (ACOTAR), was boring and relatively uneventful. Your typical YA fantasy novel, not great not terrible. (3/5) The second book A Court of Mist and Fury(ACOMAF) was bad, simply awful. Both love interests suck ass, they're possessiveness is turned into romance and I hate how mental health was villanized. Also the main character is stupid.(1/5) The third book A Courth of Wings and Ruin (ACOWAR) was tiring. SJM must think herself a god to publish and book this big and this bad. It was confusing and everything about it was convoluted and unessecary. Also the performative feminism wasn't cute.(2/5)
